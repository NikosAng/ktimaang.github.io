Our Exquisite Olive Oil

We believe that the terroir of Zakynthos island is the most crucial factor in producing an exceptional extra virgin olive oil. Our dedication to the land, combined with time-honored traditions and modern techniques, results in an olive oil of unparalleled quality and flavor.

Premium Koroneiki Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Our signature extra virgin olive oil, crafted from the finest Koroneiki olives. Harvested in November and cold-pressed within hours, this luxurious oil boasts extremely low acidity and an abundance of polyphenols. Its robust flavor profile features notes of fresh grass, green apple, and a peppery finish.

Limited Edition Unfiltered EVOO

For the true connoisseur, our unfiltered extra virgin olive oil offers an intense, full-bodied experience. This small-batch production captures the essence of our Zakynthian olive groves, with a rich, complex flavor and a slightly cloudy appearance that signifies its artisanal quality.
